About Me

Teaching Linguistics courses at the collegiate level, conducting research on Autism, doing behavior-therapy for the individuals with autism, and now UX/UI designing; In all of my past positions, my main goal has been to assist people. I believe we are here to make life easier for each other in any way that we can.

I have a UX/UI design certificate by George Washington University and a PhD in clinical Linguistics by University Kebangsaan Malaysia. I consider myself a ‘forever student’, eager to both improve my foundations in front-end development and stay in tune with the latest UX/UI designing strategies through continued coursework.

My interests are gardening, photography, jogging, and reading books. I find peace through planting flowers and taking care of them. The process of growing and changes in different seasons always inspire me in web designing. I have shared some of my pictures here.
